How It Works

  • The app determines your location via GPS.
  • Enter the courier's destination. An overview of the trip is displayed on the map.
  • Confirm your booking.
  • Wait for your courier. Your location, and the courier's, are shown on the map.
  • Optionally, extend the trip.
  • Receive your receipt and rate your courier.

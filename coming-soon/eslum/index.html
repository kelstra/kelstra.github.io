About Us

Eslum Cabs is a transport service company incorporated in 2014 and dedicated to providing personalized and quality taxi services, vehicle rental, hire and leasing services.

The company has created a niche in catering for the discerning executives, corporate entities and expatriates who value comfort and taste in a top of the range driving experience.

We provide high end 4x4 SUVs i.e. Land Cruiser Prados, minibuses and vans, 4-seater saloon cars, as well as a 24 hour taxi service anywhere within the Nairobi city and its environs.

We offer a dedicated and personalized taxi service with highly roadworthy, immaculate saloon vehicles and vans, and a pool of highly trained chauffeurs.
